featured artist : AURORA
Singer-Songwriter
Producer
AURORA (Aurora Aknes) is a Norweigan singer-songwriter and producer. Her music is widely known to have a dreamy, airy, and emotional vibe but still maintains catchy and direct. As a testament to her talent, her most popular song, Runaway, is written at the young age of 11 or 12 which is astounding for the great quality of instrumental and lyrical content in the aforementioned single.


Her most popular songs however, displays a consistent theme of environmental issues: Songs like "The Seed" and "All is Soft Inside" show the struggles of Mother Nature due to human activity.
